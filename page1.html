About Me

Cesar Marin

Cesar Marin is a Mortgage Banker with more than 12 years experience combined as a Realtor and as a mortgage loan originator. He is well-versed in a wide array of financing options, and he is here to help you solve your mortgage and real estate most pressing situations by finding the most suitable program and rate for your particular and unique scenario. As a Mortgage Consultant, he is guided by his moral principles of Integrity, Reliability and Quality, which he translates into his business practice. This his personal philosophy has allowed him to help many borrowers in almost every situation. Cesar is a member of several Associations concerned with customer satisfaction and has helped many to achieve their goal of owning a first home or getting financing for their existing home or second home, or an investment property.
His personal philosophy is to provide exceptional customer service and to take time to understand his clients' needs, to find the best mortgage for them. From first-time homebuyers to investors and experienced homebuyers, he treats each transaction as if it were his own, always keeping his clients' best interest as his top priority. Whether you're buying property for the first time, refinancing a mortgage or getting financing for your investment property, you deserve a personally tailored, customized needs-analysis solution. Cesar's professional service is founded on his firm belief that your long-term financial security and wealth accrual is better served when the financing you are trying to obtain is the result of a carefuly tailored analysis of your particular needs. Contact Cesar to tell him about your situation. He will listen to you and help you to find the best solution.
Cesar's areas of expertise are:
  1. Interest rate reduction
  2. Payment reduction
  3. Removal of MI in FHA loan
  4. Pay off of student loan
  5. Switch to Conventional w/no MI
  6. Removal of Jumbo loan
  7. Debt Consolidation
  8. Reduction of pay off years
  9. Switch to fixed rate from a variable
Cesar is a graduate of the Penn Valley College and in his spare time he is an avid reader, conversationalist, advocate, educator, and community volunteer giving back to his local neighborhood.
